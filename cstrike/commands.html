Points

  • /myskill: shows your skillpoints and rank
  • /topskill: shows the top 10
  • /rankskill: shows your rank in your chat
  • /restartskill: restarts your skillpoints after a map restart
  • /rs: resets your score

Voting

  • /nominate: nominates your chosen map [via /nominate "mapname"]
  • /rockthevote: starts a vote as early as 10 minutes before a map change

Socials

  • /discord:drops the link in chat
  • /owner: drops owners discord tag
